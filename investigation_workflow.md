# Cyber Crime Investigation Workflow

## Overview
This document outlines a structured workflow followed during cyber crime investigations, from complaint intake to case closure.

## Incident Reporting
- Registration of cyber crime complaints through official portals or police stations.
- Initial assessment to categorize the nature of the attack (fraud, phishing, identity theft).

## Preliminary Analysis
- Verification of incident details.
- Identification of affected systems, accounts, and victims.
- Preservation of volatile data where applicable.

## Technical Investigation
- Analysis of email headers, URLs, IP addresses, and logs.
- Correlation of indicators of compromise (IOCs).
- Timeline reconstruction of the attack.

## Evidence Handling
- Secure acquisition and storage of digital evidence.
- Chain of custody maintenance.
- Forensic imaging and integrity checks.

## Reporting and Documentation
- Preparation of technical findings and investigation reports.
- Coordination with legal authorities and stakeholders.
- Case documentation for future reference.

## Case Closure
- Submission of final reports.
- Lessons learned and preventive recommendations.
